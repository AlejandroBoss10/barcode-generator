How the tool works

The barcode and PDF generation happens entirely in your web browser. The text you type or scan into the tool (item codes, custom label text) is never sent to, stored on, or processed by any server. Nothing you enter into the tool is collected, logged, or transmitted anywhere.

Saved queue files

The "Save queue" feature creates a file on your own device. That file is never uploaded anywhere — it stays under your control on whatever computer or device you save it to.
